# Community and Monetization Model

MT5 Robot Lab can become a community benchmark system for MetaTrader 5 research.

MVP-010 only documents the model. It does not implement ads, payments, accounts,
uploads or a public website.

## Community Model

Future community features may include:

- public benchmark categories;
- user display names and teams;
- country or region display;
- public Champion DNA summaries;
- challenge windows for suspicious results;
- reproducibility badges;
- educational writeups for top strategies.

## Submission Ethics

Users should understand that:

- leaderboard entries are research results;
- rankings are not trading recommendations;
- high profit can come with high drawdown;
- risky tactics must be disclosed;
- future rankings should separate Wild Mode from Controlled Risk Mode.

## Free Public Layer

The public layer can remain free with:

- browseable rankings;
- public-safe result pages;
- educational docs;
- sample submissions;
- local app downloads.

## Optional Future Monetization

Possible future monetization paths:

- voluntary donations;
- sponsor slots;
- premium analytics;
- private team dashboards;
- advanced export tools;
- future ads after policy review.

No monetization is implemented in MVP-010.

## Ads Boundary

No AdSense or advertising code is included in this repository in MVP-010.

Before any future ad integration:

- review platform policies;
- keep financial claims conservative;
- avoid profit promises;
- avoid presenting rankings as investment advice;
- preserve public safety scans.

## Premium Boundary

Any premium feature should be separate from the open-source research core. The
public repository should continue to disclose its limitations and should not
hide required risk data behind paid access.
