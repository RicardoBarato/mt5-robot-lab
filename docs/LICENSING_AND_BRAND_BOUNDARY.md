# Licensing and Brand Boundary

MVP-010 prepares the future leaderboard and community model. It does not change
the project license.

## Current Boundary

This repository is public research software. It does not provide financial
advice and does not approve any robot for live trading.

## Brand Rules

Future community submissions should not imply:

- official endorsement by MT5 Robot Lab;
- certainty of profit;
- live account approval;
- broker affiliation;
- MetaQuotes affiliation;
- investment recommendation.

Allowed wording:

```text
Submitted to MT5 Robot Lab leaderboard
MT5 Robot Lab benchmark entry
Research backtest artifact
```

Avoid wording:

```text
official profitable robot
sure-win robot
approved live strategy
```

## Contribution Boundary

Future contributions should preserve:

- public-safe artifacts;
- no credentials;
- no private logs;
- no raw real-account reports;
- no hidden broker paths;
- no executable binaries.

## Future MVP-011

The next recommended MVP should formalize:

- license policy;
- contribution policy;
- trademark and naming guidance;
- community submission terms;
- monetization boundaries.
