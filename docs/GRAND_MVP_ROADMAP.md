# Grand MVP Roadmap

MT5 Robot Lab is moving away from isolated microtasks into larger, controlled
MVP batches. The goal is to build durable product surfaces instead of adding
small fragments without a long-term operating model.

## Why Grand MVPs

- Larger MVPs keep product context together.
- Each batch still remains branch-isolated and validated.
- The workflow preserves safety while allowing meaningful progress.
- The app can evolve toward a normal desktop product without requiring CLI for
  the final user.

## Execution Rhythm

Every MVP must define scope, expected files, forbidden scope, validation
commands, done criteria, risk flags and next handoff. Each MVP ends with a
branch, validation, commit, push and draft PR.
