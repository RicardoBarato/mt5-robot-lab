# Submission Terms Draft

This document is a draft. It is not final legal terms and it is not final legal
advice.

MVP-011 does not enable uploads. It defines a future policy boundary for a
possible official MT5 Robot Lab ranking service.

## Optional Upload

Future upload to an official ranking service should be optional.

The local MT5 Robot Lab app should remain usable without:

- creating an account;
- uploading results;
- joining a ranking;
- paying for a hosted service.

## Submission Requirements

Future official ranking submissions should require a validated package.

The user should submit only material they have the right to publish, including:

- public-safe Champion DNA;
- public-safe tournament summary;
- public-safe risk profile;
- public-safe configuration summary;
- hashes and validation report.

Submission packages must not contain:

- credentials;
- account numbers;
- broker server details;
- private reports;
- private logs;
- `.set` files;
- `.ex5` files;
- local Windows paths.

## Public Display

Submitted results may be displayed publicly in future rankings if accepted.

Future public display can include:

- user display name;
- team name;
- country or region;
- symbol and timeframe;
- risk mode;
- benchmark metrics;
- partial public Champion DNA;
- validation status.

Champion DNA public display may be partial in the future to balance
reproducibility, license obligations and user-controlled publication.

## Rejection Rights

The project may reject, hide or remove a submission if it is:

- invalid;
- fraudulent;
- dangerous;
- incomplete;
- misleading;
- private-data leaking;
- outside the accepted category model;
- inconsistent with package hashes;
- inconsistent with validation status.

## Benchmark Boundary

A published result is a benchmark or backtest artifact. It is not a signal, not
a financial recommendation and not live account approval.
