# Disclaimer

`MT5 Robot Lab` is research software for local MetaTrader 5 strategy testing.

Backtests are not live results. They can fail in live or demo execution because
of spread, slippage, liquidity, broker differences, execution rules, overfitting
and market-regime changes.

Nothing in this repository is financial advice, a signal service, a promise of
profitability or authorization to trade with real money.
